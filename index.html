About NGP

New Green Power is a rising star in the market of Asia.

In the yar of 2009, the establisher of New Green Power organized a team of solar power specialists, which opened up the market of solar power systems in Taiwan. In 2012, the team, now known as New Green Power company, officially entered the Japanese market. New Green Power has integrated the outstanding management teams of eco-power in Asia and carried out the one-stop service system, which contains project development, construction, operation and market capitalization.
New Green Power integrates technological schemes, controls projects and manages capital, so that investors can enter Asian market easily and be benefited stably from the professional management of the company.

New Green Power is a pioneer and a record breaker in the Asian eco-power market.

  • Since 2009, New Green Power started to participating in the development of solar power plant in Taiwan.
  • In 2012, New Green Power became the first Taiwanese solar power developer successfully entered the Japanese market.
  • In 2013, New Green Power cooperated with a listed company and began to participating in the biggest development project in Japan in which Taiwan capital has ever invested.
  • In 2014, New Green Power became the first Taiwanese company to obtain the loan support for solar power project in Mainland Japan.
